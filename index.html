Summary

I am an energetic problem solver and team player. I am an outgoing social person who cares about family, education, and healthcare. I am willing to try just about anything once and love a challenge. I feel that I can accomplish just about any task with the right tools. I also believe that society underestimates the abilities of teachers outside the classroom! I believe in the importance of collaboration in work and life in order for an individual to be successful. I would like to find a job that I love where my contribution makes a difference.
